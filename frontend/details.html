FAQs

Health Insurance Currently, I accept Blue Cross/Blue Shield, Masshealth (Wellsense/Carelon only), and Optum. This list is ever-changing, please inquire to see whether I may be in the process of accepting your insurance!
Rates If you plan to pay out of pocket, my rate for a 50-minute session is $175. I offer a sliding scale for those who may have difficulty paying my full rate. I encourage you to use the “Green Bottle” image to help you decide where you might fall on the scale. I can offer a “superbill” for those who may have Out of Network benefits coverage.
Scheduling I am generally available during some weekly evening hours, and have limited daytime availability. At the moment, my services are telehealth only.
*People interested and currently receiving therapy services are entitled by law to receiving a “Good Faith Estimate” upon request. For questions or more information about your right to a Good Faith Estimate, or how to dispute a bill, visit: www.cms.gov/nosurprises
